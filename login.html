About Our Website

Welcome to TaskFlow — your simple, fast, and distraction-free to-do list app. I built this website to help students and busy people stay organised every day. No complicated options, no confusing design — just a clean space where you can note your tasks, track your progress, and stay productive.


With a smooth interface, dark mode support, and lightweight performance, TaskFlow helps you focus on what truly matters. Whether it’s study goals, daily routines, or long-term plans, this tool is made to keep your life sorted in the easiest way possible.

Features

✔Add, Edit & Delete Tasks

Manage your tasks easily with smooth interactions.

✔ Progress Tracking

See how many tasks you’ve completed using a built-in progress bar.

✔ Dark & Light Mode

Switch effortlessly between themes for comfort and style.

✔ Clean UI / Minimal Design

A clutter-free interface designed to ensure you focus only on your tasks.

✔ Responsive Design

Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and laptop.

✔ Secure Local Storage

Your tasks are automatically saved in your browser.

✔ Fast & Lightweight

Loads instantly and doesn’t lag, even with many tasks.
